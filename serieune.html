What is Série une?


Série une emerged from a frustration.

When looking back at my photos, I always felt it did not properly encapsulate depth or distance.

I read about the parallax effect in web design and decided to apply it to some of my pictures.



How does it work?


1.Select one of the tabs.

2.Pass your mouse over the scrollbar on the right.

3.Watch the photo evolve.
